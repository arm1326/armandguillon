In this page, I talk about my professionnal project

In the future, I would like to become an Information Systems Manager. The goal in attaining this position would be to take on responsibilities and, ideally, to supervise a team of technicians. Through this role, I would be able to manage an IT infrastructure. Ultimately, my goal would be to reach a management position where I could contribute to the leadership and operation of a company, building on the skills I acquire through my experience.

Regarding my future studies, I’m not yet sure if I want to continue or not at this time. However, I am considering attending an engineering school to strengthen my skills in networking and cybersecurity, an area I greatly enjoy.

As for my ideal working conditions, I would like a position that allows me to visit other countries, which would help me develop international skills. A company car would also be ideal to facilitate my travel. Finally, I would like to work in a location close to the countryside while still remaining near a city, so that I could live in a calm environment and maintain a good work-life balance.
